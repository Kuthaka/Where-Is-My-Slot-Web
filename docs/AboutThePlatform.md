# City-Based Offer Social Media Platform (Where-Is-My-Slot)

## 1. Introduction
This project is a social media platform similar to Instagram, designed mainly for businesses to post and promote offers. Users can sign up as normal users and later convert their accounts into business profiles.

## 2. Objectives
- Provide a platform for businesses to share offers.
- Enable auto-sharing across multiple social media platforms.
- Show location-based offers to users.
- Build a centralized offer discovery system.

## 3. System Overview
The platform works like a social media feed where businesses can post images, videos, and offer details. Users can browse and explore offers based on their location.

## 4. User Roles
1. **User:** Can browse offers and convert to business.
2. **Business:** Can post offers and link social media.
3. **Admin:** Manages platform and users.

## 5. Features
### 5.1 User Registration & Login
Users can register using email or phone and log in to access the platform.

### 5.2 Business Account Conversion
Users can convert their profile into a business account to start posting offers.

### 5.3 Offer Posting
Businesses can post images, videos, and offer descriptions.

### 5.4 Multi-Platform Auto Sharing
When a business posts an offer, it is automatically shared to linked social media accounts like Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.

### 5.5 Location-Based Feed
The app detects user location and displays nearby offers based on city or area.

## 6. Benefits
**For Businesses:**
- Post once and share everywhere.
- Save time.
- Reach larger audience.

**For Users:**
- Discover nearby offers.
- Personalized experience.

## 7. Conclusion
This platform simplifies offer promotion for businesses and improves discovery for users by combining social media and location-based services.
