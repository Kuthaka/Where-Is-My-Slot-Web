# Where-Is-My-Slot 📍

**Where-Is-My-Slot** is a modern, location-based social platform that connects local businesses with customers through real-time offers and promotions.

## 🌟 What is it?
Imagine a social media feed like Instagram, but entirely focused on what's happening **near you right now**. 
*   **For Users:** It’s the easiest way to discover exclusive deals, flash sales, and events in your current city.
*   **For Businesses:** It’s a powerful marketing tool. Post an offer once on our platform, and it automatically blasts out to your linked Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok accounts.

## 🚀 Key Features
*   **City-Based Discovery:** See only what's relevant to your current location.
*   **One-Click Social Sharing:** Businesses save hours by auto-syncing their promotions across all social platforms.
*   **Rich Media Feeds:** Explore offers through high-quality photos and videos.
*   **Business Transformation:** Any user can convert their profile into a business account and start promoting.

## 🛠️ Built With
We use a high-performance, modern stack to ensure a seamless experience:
*   **Mobile:** Flutter (iOS & Android)
*   **Web:** Next.js
*   **Backend:** Node.js & Express
*   **Database:** PostgreSQL (with PostGIS for precision location tracking)
